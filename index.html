Projects

Smart-Brain (Face-Recognition) App

This project combines frontend and backend development to bring you an exciting display. A user can register a profile, which they can use to sign in. Once signed in, said user may upload a URL of a photo that contains a face. The Smart-Brain will then detect that face and highlight it using a box that surrounds the area of the face! Go ahead and try it out, and do not be afraid to play around with a fun nickname for yourself! Enjoy!

Background Generator App

Have you ever started building a project with a design and colour-scheme in mind... Only to get stumped by the possibility of having to search through and test dozens of colour-schemes? Well fear not, this app has come to your rescue! You can play around with the colour-picker to test out how a gradient background would look. And if you are not looking for anything specific and feel like having fun, simply click the "Random" button to generate random hex values for random colours!
